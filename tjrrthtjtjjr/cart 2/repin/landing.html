OUR PRODUCT RANGE

WHITE MASTERBATCHES


REPIN Masterbatches maintains over 200 white master batch formulations, some with pigment loadings up to 75 percent. Applications include utility uses and high quality specialty applications such as weatherable grades, products for extrusion coatings and molding grades for food, non food and medical uses.
Different loadings of titanium dioxide are offered as per final product application. Titanium Dioxide used is in an excellent state of dispersion giving maximum opacity, brightness & gloss at economical prices.

HIGHLIGHTS
1) Food contact acceptable grades
2) Medical use grades
3) Weather able grades
4) UV blocking grades
5) Antistatic grades
6) Engineering resin grades including GF
7) Grades for very thin films, BOPP films
8) Grades for PET Fibers
9) Highly loaded products of up to 75%
10) Specific color tones in conjunction with color pigments

BLACK MASTERBATCHES

PET MASTERBATCHES


Our PET Masterbatches are highly successful in the global market as they provide immense benefits to our customers in terms of color brilliancy and low dosage levels. The range is extensive & exhaustive meeting stringent requirements of food/packaging (FDA Approved) and automotive industries with brilliance in transparency & color strength. The colors can be formulated in a vast array of bright & rich shades that frequently cannot be achieved with traditional pigment / dye technology.


HIGHLIGHTS
1) No migration to oil, water or medicine
2) Excellent color fastness
3) UV stability
4) Granule size measuring 3mm & 1mm
5) Powder form available for ease of dispersion
6) FDA approved
7) Excellent processing stability
8) Low dosage levels of 0.1% - 4% as per final product application, feeding capabilities, machine type etc

FILLER MASTERBATCHES


Our FILLER Masterbatches are concentrates of Calcium Carbonate (CaCO3) or Talc in a virgin polymer base. They offer a convenience of incorporating CaCO3 and Talc in thermo plastics, without contamination by dust. They have perfect dispersion and optimized flow properties, ensuring easy processing.


HIGHLIGHTS
1) PP and PE woven sacks
2) Mono and multi layer films
3) Blow molding
4) Injection molding
5) Very low dust formation
6) Higher Loadability upto 50%
7) Customized grades with special colors
8) Special grades with ultra fine Calcium carbonate
9) Additive packages for increasing strength even with 
10) High loading of filler masterbatch in film and Raffia
